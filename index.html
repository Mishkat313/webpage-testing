Welcome to Dream Basant

About Us

We are the Dreamers, We are from Basant.

Basant is a small village in the district of Darbhanga, Bihar. Dream Basant Trust is a non profit organization which came into existence by Allah's grace on January 2016 by the view of youths of the village. We normally reside away from Basant (for our education and careers) and see the village once or twice in a year. We dreamt to do something beneficial for our village, and to do the same we came together and created a small organization named DREAM BASANT.

The education for the youth of Basant is not which is confined in books, but the 360o view of life. We got distant from our village but our 360o view is always with us. The mission for us is to develop our village Basant in such a way that it becomes an exemplary village in India.

We have the team of 32 people from students to working professionals from different fields which brings different expertise on the platform. With the help and support of the whole village we try to help the needy, spark enthusiasm among children to excel in education and support in the on going projects for mosques and qabristan.
